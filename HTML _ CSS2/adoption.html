Adoption

At Pet World, we do not sell dogs, cats, birds and small pets. In affiliation with Pet World Charities, we rescue over 800 pets every day through adoption. Pet World is the place to adopt.

Save an animal life, and give it love

How to Adopt


Go to contact.
Find nearest branch.
Consult your vet, and bond with the pet for few minutes.
Fill in an adoption form.
Take your pet home with you.
